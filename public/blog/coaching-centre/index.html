Bringing Silicon Valley Standards to Small-Town India

How Exameets Skillverse is changing the game in Kadapa

Published on October 2024 • 8 min read

For decades, students from smaller cities have faced a difficult choice: either compromise on quality education locally or migrate to expensive metro cities for better opportunities. Exameets Skillverse Academy in Kadapa is challenging this narrative by bringing industry-standard tech education right to students' doorsteps.

The Problem with Traditional Tech Education

Most coaching institutes in tier-2 and tier-3 cities focus heavily on theoretical knowledge without practical application. Students graduate with certificates but lack the hands-on experience that employers demand. This gap forces many to either accept lower-paying jobs or spend additional years and money in metro cities to become truly job-ready.

The founders of Exameets Skillverse—Likith Sarma Jandyala, Santhosh Kumar Anantha, Veera Prasad Kommisetty, and Venkata Sivaram Krishna Macha—recognized this problem from their own experiences in the tech industry.

A Different Approach to Learning

What sets Exameets apart is their focus on practical, industry-relevant skills over academic theory. Instead of large lecture halls, they maintain small batches of maximum 15 students, ensuring personalized attention for each learner.

The academy's modular structure includes assessments at every stage, ensuring students master each concept before moving forward. This approach, combined with unlimited retries for certifications, removes the pressure of one-time exams while maintaining quality standards.

What Makes This Model Work

Industry-Focused Curriculum

Courses are designed around current industry needs, not outdated academic syllabi. Students work on real projects that they can showcase to employers.

Affordable Local Education

By operating in Kadapa instead of expensive metro cities, the academy can offer quality education at a fraction of the cost, making it accessible to middle-class families.

Personalized Mentorship

With small batch sizes and dedicated mentors, students receive individual guidance throughout their learning journey and beyond graduation.

Practical Learning Environment

Classes are conducted in "Tinglish" for better understanding, and the focus is on building portfolios and GitHub profiles that impress employers.

Beyond Just Courses: The Complete Support System

What truly differentiates Exameets is their comprehensive support system that extends far beyond classroom learning:

  • Weekly mentorship check-ins for BTech students enrolled in the 3-year program
  • Hands-on project building with minimum 3 projects per course
  • GitHub profile development and open source contribution guidance
  • Resume building and mock interview sessions
  • Parent dashboard for progress monitoring (launching November 2025)
  • Digital certificates with industry recognition for 75%+ completion rates
  • Career guidance and industry networking opportunities
  • Flexible payment options to accommodate different financial situations

The academy also plans to launch a comprehensive student platform in November 2025, featuring assignment tracking, progress monitoring, and interactive learning modules.

The Mentorship Advantage

One of the most innovative offerings is their 3-year BTech mentorship program. This isn't just about teaching technical skills—it's about comprehensive career guidance throughout a student's college journey.

Students in this program receive regular check-ins, course discounts, and most importantly, a mentor who understands the local job market and can provide realistic career advice. This personalized approach helps students make informed decisions about their career paths while building practical skills.
